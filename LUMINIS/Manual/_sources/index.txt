Welcome!
================================
Welcome to the 2017 Orbis Challenge! 
This year's Orbis Challenge will involve creating an AI for **Luminis**, a two-player territory expansion strategy game where you grow and manoeuvre your own species of fireflies to compete for territory against your opponent on a tile-based board.

.. figure:: images/gameplay_shot.png
   :align: center
   :width: 320

.. NOTE::
  Even if you are familiar with Java or Python, you should still read the documentation. The documents contain important information about the game, grading and competition rules.
